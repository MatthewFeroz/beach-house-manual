The important part

House rules

Treat our home with the same care and respect you would your own — that's really the whole list. The details:

🌿 Please do

  • Keep to registered guests only on the property
  • Keep doors & windows closed while the A/C is running
  • Supervise children at all times — especially near the lagoon, dock & waterfront
  • Use kayaks & paddleboards at your own risk, and return them to their storage areas
  • Rinse off sand before coming inside
  • Place all trash in the designated containers
  • Report damage or maintenance issues right away
  • Lock all doors & windows when leaving

🚫 Please don't

  • Smoke indoors — this is a non-smoking property
  • Host parties, events, or large gatherings without prior approval
  • Move indoor furniture outdoors
  • Dive from the dock
  • Use the boat docked at the property — it's privately owned and not part of the rental unless arranged with the host
🌙 Quiet hours are 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM — our neighborhood is home to full-time residents and children.
